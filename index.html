    About

Dr. LI Xiaodong studied in the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University, and got his BSc degree in 2006. He became a Ph.D. student in City University of Hong Kong in 2009, supervised by Prof. DENG Xiaotie, and got his PhD degree in 2014 [Thesis].
